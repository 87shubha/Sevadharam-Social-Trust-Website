About Us

A Home For Helpless

Sevadharam social trust is able to carry out its work on ground due to the generosity of its donors. Its largest supports are individuals who donate to the elder cause from their hard-earned money. Among them are also school children who want to make a difference and are motivated by teachers and parents, to do their bit for the elderly. On the other hand, Corporate Social Responsibility has increased over the years with more and more people now choosing to do their bit for society.
