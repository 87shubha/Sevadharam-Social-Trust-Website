Cloth Distribution In Slum Areas

Many of India's poor and underprivileged lack adequate clothing, and the clothing donation not only improve the lives of under privileged families but also promotes an eco-friendly concept of recycling.In our homes, we have clothes that no longer fit us, as children grow up, they grow their clothes every year, so instead of storing or throwing away old clothes, donate used clothes because what is useless to you can be a resource for the less fortunate. Keeping these objective in mind, Sevadharam organization has been distributing clothes to the poor needy people living on the pavement, slums area since 2008, according to people of all ages, children have been distributing clothe among the elderly, women and men, Purpose of Sevadharam organization - clothes to cover the body, food to eat and house to stay (old age home) to all the needy

Birthday and Festival Celebrations

Sevadharam social trust celebrates the birthday of all the elders living in the sevadharam old age home, the elderly, patients and all service workers who are serving to senior citizens by staying in the organization is celebrated their birthday with great gaiety. Sevadharam organization prepares food, desserts, dishes are prepared according to the wish of the birthday person and along with this ordered their favorite cake the birthday is celebrated with full decoration and song music's, along with this, gift are also given to the birthday person by the members of Sevadharam patient care society.The purpose of sevadharam social trust's entire event is to give a home -like environment to the elderly, to give them happiness, so that the suffering people forgets his sorrow, pain and spends the rest of their life happily and peace.

Blanket Distribution In Slum Areas

Sevadharam social trust has been doing the work of taking care of the helpless people since the year 2008 and along with this, Sevadharam NGO is doing other charity work like Distributes food, clothes and blankets to old age people, child and all the needy families in the winter season, among the poor families living in the slum, living in the pavement, in the slums hut and along with it sevadharam organization distributes the warm winter clothes among needy families.

People who donated a lot in this work of the sevadharam patient care society is also getting support, Good hearted donors who visited to the organization and donating blankets and clothes, then Sevadharam organization distributes that blankets and clothes and other donated item among to all the slums and needy.

Food Distribution Outside Old Age Home

Sevadharam social trust provides delicious and nutritious food to all the old people and caretaker who is living inside old age home on the set timetable, giving delicious, nutritious breakfasts, lunch, dinner, tea, milk, fruits, and medicines to all the helpless senior citizens.

Along with this, dishes are prepared according to preferences of the elderly once a week.

Our aim is to gives a home like atmosphere to the elderly - Sevadharam old age home.
